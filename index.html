WHAT IS REFINEMEDIUM?

What is refinemedium?

RefineMedium is a decentralised Video on Demand (VoD) platform based on Ethereum Technology
governed by Blockchain Technology through Smart Contracts.

mission

Validation, Verification and Vindication of viewership along with a rigid,immutable and secure
ledger in order to build trust in the blockchain system.

why refineMEDIUM?


A multitude of Over The Top (OTT) video platforms for video streaming and uploading, have been burgeoning in the recent years and are highly successful in generating a humongous revenue. However, the segment is supremely-centralised with a small number of corporates controlling the entire distribution ecosystem. The aim is to transform the existing industry through path-breaking technologies and make the VoD market more transparent, secure, decentralised and thus ameliorate the social experience of customers.
